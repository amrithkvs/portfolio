Get to know me

Hey, I'm Amrith.

I'm a Canadian who grew up in Dubai and have spent the last 10 years in design, all of it self-taught. Away from the screen, I'm an avid gamer, a little obsessed with interior design, and almost always partway through a thriller novel.

Honestly, design in just about any form catches my eye. I love looking at something with a magnifying glass to understand the thinking behind it, then stepping back to appreciate how it all comes together. To me, design is the crossroads that bridges the gap for people and solves real problems.
